Immediate risks

Without MFA, a leaked or guessed password gives full access to mailbox, OneDrive and shared files — that is, to confidential customer and business data.

14 devices on Windows 10 (EOL)

These machines no longer receive security patches. Every new vulnerability stays open. Not only a technical risk — also a compliance exposure toward the data protection authority.

3 leaked credentials on the dark web
